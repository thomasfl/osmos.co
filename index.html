Osmos

The community for IT academics and the industry

Osmosis /ɒzˈməʊsɪs/ - the process of gradual or unconscious assimilation of ideas, knowledge, etc.

Our mission is to facilitate IT innovation by closing the knowledge and communication gap between industry and academia by establishing a collaborative network with actors from both sides of the divide.

Our network will permit profound bilateral collaboration, aimed at technology and knowledge transfer carefully calibrated to specific organizational context and need. NETWORK OF IT ACADEMICS AND PRACTITIONNERS
